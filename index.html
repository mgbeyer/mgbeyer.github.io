Welcome to my virtual ragbag of personal information

Hi, I'm Michael G. Beyer, software engineer by profession, photographer by obsession and disorganized person by confession.
I have a broad range of interests which led to various and quite different kinds of online activities and projects over the last couple years. Widespread projects. Sort of chaotic and confusingly distributed projects. So I needed a place, a focal point, to provide interested parties with an overview and a stepping stone. Jobwise but also very personally, related to my hobbies and other topics I'm interested in.
So, come on in, be informed, have fun!


Professional showplace

I started my career as a software developer in the late 90s and I have worked on many web application projects ever since. I hold a diploma in computer science ("Diplom-Informatiker (FH)") from the university of applied sciences in Hannover, Germany (more than a BSc. degree, less than a MSc.).
Technology wise I'm a "backend guy", at home with web application frameworks and relational databases. In addition there's always a pinch of frontend design here and there, mostly based on JQuery and CSS. Professionally I'm used to Java related frameworks and - more recently - Ruby on Rails. Personally I'm more into PHP... it just happend over time :)
